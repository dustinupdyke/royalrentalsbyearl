  1. Do I need a Passport?

    As of January 23, 2007, all persons, including U.S. citizens, traveling by air between the U.S. and Canada, Mexico and the Caribbean will be required to present a valid passport.  If you do not have a passport, please contact a passport acceptance facility in your area immediately.  Do not delay.  Do it today.  It could take up to 6 weeks or longer for processing.

  3. What is included in a rental unit at the Royal Resorts?

    Royal resorts units are referred to as flexivillas. Each villa has 2 bedrooms, 2 full bathrooms, a fully-equipped kitchen, living/ dining room, & balcony. Occupancy: No more than 6 people. A villa can be divided into a suite or lock off room. The lock off room includes a room with 2 double beds and bath similar to a hotel room. The lock off room does have a small balcony. Occupancy: No more than 2 people. The suite would include the kitchen, dining/ living room, and one separate master bedroom and bathroom. Occupancy: No more than 4 people.
