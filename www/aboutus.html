About Royal Rentals by Earl

After traveling several years to different vacation destinations, my wife Sandi and I had experienced a variety of vacation hotspots. In our experience, nothing compared to the quality, service, experience and people at the Royal Resorts in Cancun. During a 1989 vacation to Cancun, we decided to buy a villa and plan a yearly vacation to the Royal resorts. One year later we owned 4 weeks.

The Royal Resorts in Cancun are comprised of the Royal Sands, Royal Islander, Royal Caribbean, Royal Mayan, and The Royal Cancun formally Vacation Club International. The Royal Hacienda is located in Playa del Carmen.

Since 1991, Sandi & I have been going to Cancun for several weeks each year. During this time, we have developed a network of approximately 8,000 owners and renters that we have helped over the years. Since then, we have rented over 3,500 villas between Christmas & Easter. We buy, sell, exchange and rent villas exclusively at the Royal Resorts in Cancun and Playa del Carmen. We have a variety of weeks and locations available all the time. We receive numerous phone calls and emails everyday.

Royal Rentals by Earl is a full time business incorporated in Pennsylvania. We are not affiliated with the Royal Resorts in Cancun; however we are a recognized rental company. We do have a contract with them. All of our rentals are assigned their confirmation numbers.
