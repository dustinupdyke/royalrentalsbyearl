About Royal Rentals by Earl

After traveling several years to different vacation destinations, my wife Sandi and I had experienced a variety of vacation hotspots. In our experience however, nothing compared to the quality, location, and people at the Royal Resorts in Cancun. During a vacation in 1989, we decided to buy a villa and plan a yearly vacation to Cancun.

The Royal Resorts in Cancun are comprised of the Royal Sands, Royal Islander, Royal Caribbean, Royal Mayan, Royal Haciendas and Vacation Club International.

My wife Sandi & I have been going to Cancun for several weeks each year since 1991. During this time we have developed a network of approximately 6,000 owners and renters that we assist. Since 1998, we have rented over 2,000 villas between Christmas & Easter. We buy, sell, exchange and rent villas exclusively at the Royal Resorts in Cancun. We have a variety of weeks and locations available all the time. We receive numerous phone calls and emails everyday.

Royal Rentals by Earl is a full time business incorporated in Pennsylvania. We are not affiliated with the Royal Resorts in Cancun.
